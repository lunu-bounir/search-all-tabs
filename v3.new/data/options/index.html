Exclusion List

Comma-separated list of keywords to exclude from tab indexing. If a tab's URL contains any of these keywords, it will be ignored by the indexer. This feature is useful for permanently excluding specific webpages from being indexed by the extension.

Browser History Search

Enable searching through your browser history in addition to active tabs.

Note: Indexing browser history will fetch page content from the internet, which may take time and bandwidth. History items are indexed only once and cached locally.
